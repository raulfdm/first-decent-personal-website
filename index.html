About

My first contact with development was when I was 14. I decided to create my own MMORPG (Tibia) server, customizing all game options and configurations, server and doing deployment from my code. In a few weeks, I had more than 150 players enojoying it. Nevertheless, I had no idea what could I do with technology and what it could offer to me, than just I give up.

At the age of 21, I've enrolled myself in production engineer degree, where I had classes related by development (algorithm and basic of development) and it was enough to change my whole life. My professor saw my feelings and happiness to do those classes and invite me to change my degree to Systems Information. Of course, I did it!

At university, I've started to learn about data structuring, C#, Java, PHP, Android when I met JavaScript and web technologies and I decided to go deep and increasily learn more and more about technology. Unfortunally it is impossible to focus on everything, than I decied to focus on JavaScript and web world!

I've always been gaming addicted. But today I know that I've found the best, the most challenging and the funniest game of all time: Coding!
